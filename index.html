Having scaled multiple companies from early stage to market leaders, I know the pattern: the difference between exponential growth and plateau comes down to a handful of critical decisions made at exactly the right moment.

I partner with founders and executives to engineer the systems, teams, and strategies that turn growth obstacles into structural advantages. But scaling isn't just an operational challenge. It's deeply personal.

The isolation of leadership. The weight of decisions that keep you up at night. The moments when you're not sure if you're making the right call, and there's no one you can really talk to about it. I've been there, and I'm here to listen when the personal burden of leadership becomes too much to carry alone.
